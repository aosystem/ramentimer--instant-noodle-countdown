# Ramen Timer: Instant Noodle Companion 🍜⏱️

A specialized, ultra-fast **Countdown Timer** built with **Flutter**, specifically designed for the perfect preparation of instant cup noodles. This app focuses on speed and simplicity, ensuring you never overcook your noodles again.

## 🌟 Key Features
- **Instant Start:** Begins measuring immediately upon launch to save you time.
- **One-Tap Adjustments:** Quickly change duration to match different noodle types (3-min, 5-min, etc.).
- **Visual Themes:** Multiple design themes to suit your style and mood.
- **Minimalist UX:** A hassle-free interface that lets you focus on your meal.
- **Global Support:** Fully localized in **50+ languages**.

## 💡 Best For...
- **Daily Convenience:** Perfect for busy students and office workers.
- **Noodle Enthusiasts:** Ensuring every cup of ramen is served at its peak texture.
- **Simplicity Lovers:** Anyone who prefers a dedicated, streamlined tool over complex kitchen timers.

## 🛠 Technical Overview
- **Framework:** Flutter
- **Language:** Dart
- **Logic:** Efficient lifecycle-aware timer implementation and dynamic theme state management.

*Note: This repository contains the core logic and source code. Assets (images, fonts, and localization files) are excluded to protect intellectual property.*
